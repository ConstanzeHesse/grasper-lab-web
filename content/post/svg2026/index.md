The Scottish Vision Group Meeting 2026 took place in Nairn, Scotland from April 10th-12th, and Keith, Martin, and Daniela presented some of our recent (and very exciting) work.
<!--more--> 
Keith's talk presented data showing that discriminative and affective dimensions of touch shape contact force during free exploration. 
Daniela's talk on approach biases towards real-food and food-images suggests that approach biases seem largely insensitive to low-level perceptual stimulus features. 
And last but not least, Martin showed in his talk that illumination and contrast have distinct effects on obstacle avoidance movements.
